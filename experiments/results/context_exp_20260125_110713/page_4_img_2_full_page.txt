This document serves as a technical instruction manual detailing the process for onboarding a Panasonic Air Conditioner (AC) into a smart home application environment. It addresses two distinct scenarios: Matter Certified devices and proprietary Non-Matter devices.

---

## Technical Interpretation

### 1. What is the PURPOSE of this screen/diagram?

The purpose is to provide a comprehensive, step-by-step guide for the initial setup and network pairing (onboarding) of Panasonic Air Conditioners. It outlines two specific procedures depending on the AC's compatibility standard:

1.  **Connecting a Matter Certified AC** to a third-party Matter Ecosystem Application.
2.  **Connecting a Non-Matter AC** to the proprietary **MirAle Application**.

### 2. What ACTION should the user take?

The user must first identify which category their Air Conditioner falls into (Matter Certified or Non-Matter) and follow the corresponding steps:

**If the AC is Matter Certified (Procedure A):**
1.  **Verification:** Check the Ecosystem Application to ensure it supports the "Matter Room Air Conditioner" device type.
2.  **Device Setup:** Power the AC OFF, then back ON.
3.  **Activate Setup Mode:** Press the "SMART" button on the remote control until the WiFi Indicator on the AC begins blinking.
4.  **App Onboarding:** Follow the specific setup instructions provided within the Ecosystem Application. *(Crucial Note: Ensure the connection is on the 2.4GHz frequency band.)*

**If the AC is Non-Matter (using the MirAle App) (Procedure B):**
1.  **Enable Prerequisites:** Ensure Wi-Fi and Location services are enabled on the smartphone.
2.  **Initiate Pairing:** Click the **+** (plus) icon located on the app dashboard (home screen) to add a new device.
3.  **Select Device Type:** On the "Select device" screen, scroll to the "Appliances" category and select **Air Conditioner**.

### 3. What PROBLEM does this help solve?

This guide solves the technical problem of **initial device pairing (onboarding)**. Specifically, it resolves issues where a user is unable to:
*   Link a physical Panasonic AC unit to a digital control application (MirAle or a Matter ecosystem app).
*   Put the AC unit into the correct network configuration (setup mode) required for wireless detection and provisioning.
