Bluetooth
Bluetooth is a wireless technology used for exchanging data between devices over short distances.
The Bluetooth function of your mobile device can be used to connect to the camera and send it
network information.
WPS
WPS is a function of wireless routers that is used to easily connect wireless devices to wireless
routers. A WPS button on a wireless router indicates that it has the WPS function.
