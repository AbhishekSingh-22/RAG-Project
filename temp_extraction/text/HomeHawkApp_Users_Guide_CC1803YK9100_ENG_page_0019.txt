I only want to receive notifications when motion is detected
for people
You can configure the window camera to notify your mobile device only when motion is detected for a
person and not for objects.
Supported devices
R Window camera (KX-HNC500, KX-HNC505)
Required procedures
R Use the [HomeHawk] app to set [Send Notifications] to [Person Only]. See  “Once motion is
detected”, Page 107.
